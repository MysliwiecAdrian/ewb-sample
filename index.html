Who We Are

CCNY is a student chapter dedicated to using engineering to create sustainable solutions for communities in need. Our members design, collaborate, and implement real-world projects that make a global impact.

Community Impact

Partnering with communities to address critical needs through innovative and sustainable design.

Student Empowerment

Providing hands-on experience for future engineers to apply classroom knowledge to global challenges.

Collaboration

Working with professionals, mentors, and local partners to ensure long-term project success.

Our Projects

Explore some of our ongoing and past initiatives that bring sustainable engineering to life.

Clean Water Access

Designing sustainable water systems to provide safe drinking water to rural communities.

Bridge Construction

Improving accessibility and safety through community-built bridges.

Solar Energy Systems

Delivering renewable power solutions for schools and clinics in underserved regions.
